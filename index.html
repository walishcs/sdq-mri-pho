Māori's Long Vowels

In te reo Māori, long and short Vs make phonemic contrast:
  • kēkē ‘armpit’ ∼ keke ‘cake’
  • kākā ‘bird species’ ∼ kaka ‘garment’
  • kōkō ‘tui, bird species’ ∼ koko ‘shovel’
  • kīkī ‘speak’ ∼ kiki ‘kick’
  • kūkū ‘pigeon’ ∼ kuku ‘mussel’
